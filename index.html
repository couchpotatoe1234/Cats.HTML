About Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility, playfulness, and ability to hunt vermin. Cats have been domesticated for thousands of years and are one of the most popular pets worldwide.

Different Types of Cats

Simply enough, there are a whole lot of cat types. There are 73 official cat breeds according to The International Cat Association. There are also over 40 different "genetic crosses" between species of cats according to Genetic Analysis of Feline Interspecies Hybrids. That adds up to a whole lot of cats!
