Siberian

The Siberian cat is a longhair with a very distinct and thick triple coat. They are also very intelligent and curious about their surroundings. They are usually very quiet and don't talk much (mine do). They mature very slowly and reach their full size in 3 years. Their fur also changes with the temperatures, from thick dense fur in the winter, to thinner fur in the summer. They are very easygoing and sociable. ↓↓↓These are my cats Lily and Panda ↓↓↓
