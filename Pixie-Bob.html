Pixie-Bob

The Pixie-Bob cat comes as both a shorthair and a longhair and is very playful and fun. These cats in fact, even look a bit like bobcats. They are particularly fond of water unlike many other cats. These cats often have lynx-tipped ears and they have bobbed tails meaning that they are short. These cats also can have polydactl toes, maning that they have extra toes, up to seven!
