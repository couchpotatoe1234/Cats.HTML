Scottish Fold & Scottish Straight

The Scottish Fold, like their close relatives the Scottish Straight, come as both a longhair and a shorhair, with The Scottish Folds having unique folded back ears, explaining their name. They all have large round eyes and are often described as looking 'round'. They are also rather affectionate and very intelligent. The Folds are in fact born with straight ears and the folds will only appear after a little while. The ones that remain with straight ears keep them as a Scottish Straight.
