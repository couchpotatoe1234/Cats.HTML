American Shorthair & American Wirehair

The American Shorthair and the American Wirehair are very similar in personality and behavior, with the main defining difference between them being their fur. The American Shorthair has short hair, while the American Wirehair has a hard, wiry coat. Both breeds are very chill, easygoing and very playful. They don't talk much, rather doing a "silent meow" with their mouths open as a form of greeting or request.
