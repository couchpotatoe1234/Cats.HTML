Turkish Angora

The Turkish Angora cat is a longhair with a love for high places. They are highly intelligent, so intelligent in fact, that their owners have even called them "Einsteins", yet some might fail to remember even simple things. They are very affectionate and loving and will act like a dog, greeting someone at the front door. They have almond-shaped eyes that come in a variety of colors including blue, green, and amber. They can even have odd eyes meaning that their eyes are different colors.
