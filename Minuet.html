Minuet

The Minuet cat has many different types, including Minuet longhair, Minuet shorthair, Minuet Talls longhair, and Minuet Talls shorthair. The Minuet cat has very defining short legs and a round face, while the Minuet Talls is pretty much the same, just with long legs. They are also all very playful and very people oriented.
